world s earliest civilizations in the fertile basin of the yellow river in the north china plain for millennia china s political system was based on hereditary monarchies or dynasties beginning with the semi-legendary xia dynasty since then china has expanded fractured and re-unified numerous times in 1912, the republic of china roc replaced the last dynasty and ruled the chinese mainland until 1949, when it was defeated by the communist people s liberation army in the chinese civil war the communist party established the people s republic of china in beijing on 21 september 1949, while the roc government retreated to taiwan with its present de facto capital in taipei both the roc and prc continue to claim to be the legitimate government of all china though the latter has more
