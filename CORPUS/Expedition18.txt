march 2009 debris incident edit on 12 march 2009, a piece of debris from the upper stage of a delta ii rocket used to launch a gps satellite in 1993, passed close to the iss the conjunction between the debris and the space station was not detected until it was too late to perform a collision avoidance manoeuvre the crew prepared to evacuate the station by closing hatches between modules and boarding the soyuz spacecraft that was docked to provide emergency crew escape 3 the debris did not hit the station instead it passed by at 16 38 utc and the crew were cleared to resume operations
