tropical storm nina typhoon nina known in the philippines as typhoon bebeng was the fourth-deadliest tropical cyclone on record approximately 229,000 people died after the banqiao dam in zhumadian henan province china collapsed and devastated areas downstream the collapse of the dam due to heavy floods also caused a string of smaller dams to collapse adding more damage by the typhoon typhoon
